## The problem

Evaluating market risk across an index by hand doesn't scale. I wanted a single,
reproducible pipeline that could ingest years of price history and output a clear,
ranked picture of risk and return across every constituent.

## What I built

A fully automated risk-analysis pipeline processing **10+ years of NIFTY 50** historical
data. It computes daily and annualised returns, volatility, and Sharpe ratios across all
50 constituent equities, then renders a production-style dashboard.

- **Returns & volatility** computed per equity, annualised for comparability.
- **Risk-adjusted ranking** via Sharpe ratio, surfacing the top decile of assets.
- **Distribution analysis** with kernel density estimates (KDE) and Gaussian fits to
  map outliers and volatility clustering.
- **Sector benchmarking** laid out with Matplotlib **GridSpec** for a dense, readable view.

## Why it matters

The output turns a wall of price data into an actionable ranking — which names carry the
best risk-adjusted return, and which are outliers worth a second look.

## Stack & techniques

Python, Pandas, NumPy, SciPy, Matplotlib (GridSpec), statistical comparison models,
Gaussian distribution fitting, percentile ranking.
